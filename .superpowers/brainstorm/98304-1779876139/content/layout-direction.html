A — Map Dominant

~80% map, slim side panel for alerts and asset status. Maximum geographic awareness, minimal distraction. Best for ops staff watching incidents unfold in real time.

B — Split View

~60% map, ~40% data panels. Balanced — map for spatial awareness, panels for incident feed, fire danger ratings, at-risk assets, and crew status. Best for decision-makers who need context alongside the map.

C — Command Grid

Dashboard-style grid — map takes top-left 2/3, with KPI widgets, incident tiles, fire danger, weather, and crew status as a mosaic. Executive/showcase-friendly, impressive at a glance. Best for demonstrations and leadership briefings.
